About OEMUN

Building leaders through diplomacy and dialogue

Our Mission

OEMUN strives to provide high school and university students with an authentic Model United Nations experience. We foster critical thinking, public speaking, and diplomatic skills while addressing real-world global challenges. Our conference promotes international cooperation, cultural understanding, and leadership development.
